# Hidayah AI - 30-Day Ramadan Research Companion
**Vision:** An agentic Quranic research tool that syncs Arabic, Urdu, and English audio/text.
**Core Features:**
- 30 Para (Juz) Navigation.
- Multilingual Sync (Arabic + Urdu/English).
- "AI Scholar" Agent: Uses Gemini 1.5 for reasoning and Tavily for web search.
- RAG: Upload scholarly PDFs to ask context-aware questions.
